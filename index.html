About Me

I’m a Master’s student in Computing Science at Simon Fraser University, advised by Dr. Keval Vora. My research focuses on disaggregated memory, particularly RDMA-based synchronization techniques. Prior to graduate school, I was a Senior Software Engineer at Enmotech, where I worked on distributed storage systems. I also have experience in embedded systems, including Ethernet switches and industrial motion controllers.
